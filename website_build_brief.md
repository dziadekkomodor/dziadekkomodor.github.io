# Website Build Brief — Index

Full business/content brief for Dziadek Komodor (PC/electronics repair, Piła/Bydgoszcz, Poland). Split
into focused files so only the relevant part loads for a given task — don't read all three unless doing
a full content pass.

- **`brief/personas-pricing.md`** — business context (legal status, service area), target personas, top
  customer objections, required page content (pricing, warranty, process, FAQ, contact form),
  competitive landscape, open items before build. Read when writing copy, structuring pages, or
  targeting a specific persona.
- **`brief/design-tech.md`** — design direction (palette, type, imagery, layout, motion), technical
  requirements (performance, accessibility, security, GDPR, structured data/JSON-LD templates,
  AI/answer-engine content structuring). Read when implementing a page template, schema markup, or a
  compliance-related feature.
- **`brief/launch-checklist.md`** — Stage 2 (GitHub Pages) and Stage 3 (domain, email, Discord webhook,
  Google Business Profile, directory listings, search engine submission, reviews). Only needed at launch
  time, not during normal build work.

*Reflects research and decisions made as of mid-2026. Prices, thresholds, and legal figures reflect 2026
Polish rules at time of writing — verify before publishing anything as final, especially pricing and
legal/compliance text.*
